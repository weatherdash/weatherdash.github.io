Weather Impact Dashboard Information

At-a-glance weather for every site in the selected line of business. Daily focuses on current conditions; 10-Day flags conditions expected during the next 10 days.

Daily View

Sites are flagged when current conditions meet these rules.

RED — Severe Impact
  • Temperature < 10°F
  • Freezing precipitation
  • More than 3″ snow in 72 hrs
  • Severe storms
YELLOW — Minor Impact
  • Light snow
  • Fog / visibility issues
  • Rain + freezing-risk temps
  • Any active NWS alert
GREEN — No Impact

No issues.

10-Day View

Sites are flagged when forecast conditions meet these rules.

RED — Severe Weather
  • Freezing precipitation
  • Heavy snow or storms
  • Temperatures ≤ 10°F
YELLOW — Moderate Risk
  • Light snow
  • Fog
  • Freeze-risk temps + rain
  • Any active NWS alert
GREEN — No Expected Issues
